Help

  1. Question: How to purchase tickets?

    Answer: Go to the "Buy Tickets" page, select the museum and the date of your visit, and proceed to checkout.

  2. Question: How to view artifacts?

    Answer: Go to the "View All" page, select the civilization you are interested in, and enjoy the virtual tour.

  3. Question: How to contact us?

    Answer: Go to the "Contact Us" section and fill out the form. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

About Us

Pharaoh's Palette is a platform that allows you to explore and learn about ancient artifacts from various civilizations including
